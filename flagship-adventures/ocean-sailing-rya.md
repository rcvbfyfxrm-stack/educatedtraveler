## The Certifications

**RYA (Royal Yachting Association)** credentials are the most respected sailing qualifications worldwide. Recognized in 50+ countries, they're your passport to charter boats, crew positions, and command.

| Certification | Duration | Outcome |
|---------------|----------|---------|
| Competent Crew | 5 days | Crew safely on any yacht |
| Day Skipper | 10 days | Skipper in familiar waters by day |
| Coastal Skipper | 14 days | Skipper coastal passages, day and night |
| Yachtmaster Offshore | Varies | Command vessels up to 200nm offshore |

## The Waters

**Greece (Aegean Sea)**
The birthplace of Western sailing. Island-hop through the Cyclades, navigating the same waters Odysseus sailed. Perfect conditions for learning—reliable winds, countless anchorages, stunning beauty.

**Croatia (Adriatic Sea)**
Over a thousand islands. Complex navigation, tidal considerations, and the notorious Bora wind. The ideal training ground for advanced skills.

**Atlantic Ocean**
The classic trade wind route. Canary Islands to the Caribbean—2,800 nautical miles of open ocean. The proving ground for offshore sailors since Columbus.
