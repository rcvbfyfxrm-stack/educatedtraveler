How we work with instructors

We're not looking for contractors to fill slots. We're looking for partners who want to build something meaningful together.

You bring: Your expertise, your methodology, your passion for teaching people who actually want to go deep.

We bring: The students (pre-qualified, committed), the logistics (venue, meals, transport), and the structure that lets you focus entirely on teaching.

Together: We create experiences that people talk about for years.
