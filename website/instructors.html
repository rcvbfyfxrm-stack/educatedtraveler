For Instructors & Practitioners

We're building something together.

A community of instructors who believe transformation happens when you give it time, place, and the right people.

There's a reason yoga evolved in India, sailing mastery developed in the Mediterranean, and wine culture took root in Tuscany. Some things need to be learned at the source.

We're building EducatedTraveler around a simple belief: real transformation requires time. Not a weekend. Not a week. Enough time to truly immerse, to form bonds that last, and to emerge changed.

If that resonates with how you teach, we'd love to connect.

What we're creating

Extended immersive programs—21 days to 6 months—where small groups (8-12 people) earn real certifications while living at the source of their craft.

Our programs are built around three rhythms:

Adventure

Movement, exploration, shared challenges. The body needs to be engaged for the mind to open.

Immersion

Deep practice, real credentials, learning from those who've dedicated their lives to the craft.

Optimization

Rest, reflection, integration. Space to absorb what's being learned without burning out.

How we work with instructors

We're not looking for contractors to fill slots. We're looking for partners who want to build something meaningful together.

You bring: Your expertise, your methodology, your passion for teaching people who actually want to go deep.

We bring: The students (pre-qualified, committed), the logistics (venue, meals, transport), and the structure that lets you focus entirely on teaching.

Together: We create experiences that people talk about for years.

The practical side

— Cohorts are small (8-12) and students have invested significantly to be there
