01
RYA Day Skipper → Yachtmaster · Certificate of Competence

Skipper a yacht on open water

UKSA — Cowes, Isle of Wight. The largest RYA training centre in the world, teaching on the Solent. From your first week afloat to the 16-week professional route, every rung is the same ladder the industry climbs.

In your futureThe RYA ticket is what the sailing world hires on — charter skipper, delivery crew, or a boat of your own taken somewhere real.

02
WSET Levels 1–3 → Diploma · Awards in Wines

Taste wine the way the trade does

WSET School London — Bermondsey. The flagship classroom of the Wine & Spirit Education Trust, the qualification the wine world reads at a glance. Level 3 runs as a six-day intensive, Saturdays, or evenings.

In your futureWSET compounds: Level 2 in days, Level 3 in a season, the Diploma beyond — currency in restaurants, import, retail and auction rooms everywhere.

London · UK3 days → 6-day intensiveschool ↗dossier →
03
International Sushi Chef Certificate

Make sushi where sushi lives

Tokyo Sushi Academy — Tsukiji, Tokyo. Japan's first sushi school, steps from the old fish market, teaching Edomae technique in English. The certificate takes attendance and a passed nigiri and hosomaki skill test — it is not given, it is earned.

In your futureIts international graduates cook in more than fifty countries. The paper says you trained at the source, and the hands prove it.

Tokyo · Japan10 days → 5 weeksschool ↗dossier →
04
Apnea Academy · Levels 1–3

Hold your breath and go quietly deep

Apnea Academy — Mediterranean & Red Sea. The school founded in 1995 by Umberto Pelizzari, seventeen-time world record holder, in the lineage of Mayol and Maiorca. Three levels, taught in the water; its instructors are certified by Pelizzari himself.

In your futureDepth, calm and safety that carry into everything else you do at sea — and the first rungs of the instructor ladder, if you want them.

Italy · Egyptlevel by levelschool ↗dossier →
05
GIP Certificate → International Technical Degree

Compose a fragrance in Grasse

Grasse Institute of Perfumery — Provence. Ten students per class, taught in English, inside a listed 13th-century convent in the town the perfume industry still answers to. Summer school sessions run June to September.

In your futureThe one-to-two-week summer school is the short road into the capital of perfume; the technical degree is the long one, into its labs.

Grasse · France1–2 weeks → degreeschool ↗dossier →
06
NOLS Wilderness First Responder · WFR

Be the one who knows what to do, days from help

NOLS — Lander, Wyoming & worldwide. The school that has defined outdoor leadership since 1965. The WFR is 80 hours over nine to ten days: patient assessment, evacuation calls, real scenarios in real weather.

In your futureThe minimum ticket for guiding work almost anywhere wild — and the person every expedition wants along when something goes wrong.

Wyoming · worldwide9–10 daysschool ↗dossier →
07
Le Grand Diplôme® · Cuisine + Pâtisserie

Rebuild your cooking on French technique

Le Cordon Bleu — Paris. The name every kitchen on earth recognises. Nine months, Basic to Superior, across both cuisine and pâtisserie — demonstration by the chef, then the same dish from your own hands, graded.

In your futureStill the most recognised line a cook can put on a CV. The heavyweight of this list, in time and in weight of paper.

Paris · France9 monthsschool ↗dossier →
08
Wat Pho Certificate · Ministry-recognised school

Learn nuad Thai inside the temple that codified it

Wat Pho Thai Traditional Medical School — Bangkok. Founded in 1955 within the temple of the Reclining Buddha, on a lineage kept since 1836 — the first Thai medical school approved by the Ministry of Education. Certificates follow set hours and a passed final exam.

In your futureThe school name spa and bodywork worlds everywhere know on sight, from short courses up to the 200-hour professional program.

SCA Coffee Skills Program · Foundation → Professional

Pull espresso to a world standard

Prufrock Barista Training Centre — Leather Lane, London. A dedicated training floor under one of the rooms that made London specialty coffee, with authorised SCA trainers running Barista Skills from Foundation to Professional.

In your futureSCA certificates are the coffee world's shared currency — the same modules are read the same way in Melbourne, Oslo and New York.

Vakuum Certificate · Lab Week 01 — the one we run

A week in the modernist lab, beside the master

Vakuum by Martin Lippo — Barcelona. A working technique lab — vacuum, low temperature, nitrogen, textures — run by a master the founder knows in person. One week in October, a small cohort, and a certificate in your name from the master's own school.
