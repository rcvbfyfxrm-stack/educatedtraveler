Schools in London / Roehampton

  • Society of Scribes and Illuminators (SSI)
    Creative Development Course
    The Society of Scribes and Illuminators, founded in 1921 and based at the Art Workers' Guild in London, runs a three-year part-time Creative Development Course guiding calligraphers toward their own personal direction under SSI Fellows.
    https://calligraphyonline.org/
  • Calligraphy & Lettering Arts Society (CLAS)
    Festival of Calligraphy (Summer School)
    The Calligraphy & Lettering Arts Society is a UK lettering-arts organisation founded in 1994 that holds an annual week-long residential Summer School (Festival of Calligraphy) of workshops with leading tutors.
    https://www.clas.co.uk/
  • City Lit (Calligraphy & Lettering)
    Calligraphy and illumination (three-term programme)
    City Lit is a central London adult-education college whose calligraphy department offers a three-term programme covering broad-edged scripts, illumination, and gold-leaf work taught by SSI-Fellow tutors.
    https://www.citylit.ac.uk/courses/art-and-design/contemporary-crafts/calligraphy
