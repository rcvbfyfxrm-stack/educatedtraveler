Schools in Jingdezhen

  • Jingdezhen Ceramic University
    International Ceramic Art Work Camp / summer training college
    Jingdezhen Ceramic University is China's only multidisciplinary university named after ceramics, running degree programs and short International Ceramic Art Work Camps that immerse overseas students in traditional Chinese porcelain technique and design.
    https://www.jcu.edu.cn/english/
  • The Pottery Workshop Jingdezhen (residency & courses)
    Jingdezhen International Residency (residency studio)
    The Pottery Workshop's Jingdezhen hub offers an international ceramic residency with on-campus studio and housing plus year-round workshops and master classes, embedding artists in the city's porcelain-craft networks.
    https://pws-hub.com/
  • Sanbao International Ceramic Art Village
    Sanbao ceramic residency (one to six months)
    Founded in 1995 by ceramic artist Li Jianshen in the Sanbao valley outside Jingdezhen, the village runs a full ceramic-artist residency of roughly one to six months that hosts dozens of domestic and international makers each year for creative exchange.
    https://www.facebook.com/SanbaoCeramic/
