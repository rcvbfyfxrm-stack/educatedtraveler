Where it is taught — hand-verified

Schools in Paris

  • Le Cordon Bleu Paris
    Diplôme de Cuisine
    The classic three-tier French cuisine diploma — Basic, Intermediate and Superior — taught through chef demonstrations followed by individual hands-on practice.
    https://www.cordonbleu.edu/paris/cuisine-diploma/en
  • FERRANDI Paris
    Intensive Professional Program in French Cuisine
    Paris culinary school whose 16-week English-taught Intensive Professional Program in French Cuisine teaches classical and regional technique with restaurant service and market excursions before a 3-6 month internship.
    https://www.ferrandi-paris.com/en/french-cuisine-programs/fundamentals-of-french-cooking-professionnal-program
  • École Ducasse
    Culinary Arts Diploma
    Alain Ducasse's culinary school, with its Paris-area campus in Meudon, running a nine-month intensive Culinary Arts Diploma for career-changers that pairs hands-on French technique with management and entrepreneurial modules.
    https://www.ecoleducasse.com/en/career-changers-culinary-arts
