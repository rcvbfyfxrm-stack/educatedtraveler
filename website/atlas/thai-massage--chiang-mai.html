Schools in Chiang Mai

  • Old Medicine Hospital Thai Massage School Shivagakomarpaj (OMH)
    Thai Massage Foundations & Therapeutic Levels 1-4
    Training at the 1962-founded school that is the birthplace of Northern-style Thai massage in Chiang Mai.
    https://www.oldmedicine.org/courses
  • Old Medicine Hospital (Thai Massage School Shivagakomarpaj / OMH)
    Foundation / Northern-Style Thai Massage certificate courses
    A Chiang Mai Thai-massage school founded in 1962 and registered under the Thai Ministry of Education, widely regarded as the source of Northern-style Thai massage, offering multi-day hands-on certificate courses.
    https://www.oldmedicine.org/
  • ITM - International Training Massage School
    Certified Advanced Thai Massage Professional (CATP), six-level program
    A Chiang Mai vocational Thai-massage school offering six progressive levels from basic to advanced Nuad Boran, with a hands-on practicum-heavy curriculum culminating in the Certified Advanced Thai Massage Professional credential.
    https://www.itmthaimassage.com/
  • Sunshine Massage School
    Beginner's Traditional Thai Massage Course (2 weeks / 60 hours) plus advanced courses
    A Chiang Mai school recognised by the Thai Ministries of Education and Health that runs a structured two-week, 60-hour beginner's Traditional Thai Massage course with a certificate of completion, plus advanced specialty courses.
    https://www.sunshine-massage-school.com/
  • Chetawan (Wat Pho) Thai Traditional Massage School, Chiang Mai branch
    Professional Thai Massage for Health (165 hours) / 200-hour Therapy program
    The Chiang Mai branch of the Wat Pho/Chetawan school network, offering the Wat Pho lineage's longer professional Thai massage programs including a 165-hour Professional Thai Massage for Health and a 200-hour therapy course.
    https://watpomassage.com/en/branch/detail?id=259
