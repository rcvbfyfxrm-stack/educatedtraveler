Schools in Sierra de Guara (Alquézar & Rodellar)

  • Terres d'Aventure (Terdav)
    Aventures canyon en Sierra de Guara (7-day canyoning trip)
    A seven-day guided canyoning trip based in the Rodellar valley at the heart of Sierra de Guara, Europe's classic canyoning region.
    https://www.terdav.com/ps-espagne/rn-pyrenees-espagnoles/tp-voyage-famille/at-canyoning/espf20--aventures-canyon-sierra-guara
  • Expediciones Sierra de Guara
    Week of canyoning x5
    A guiding company running canyoning in the Sierra de Guara since 1978, with a five-day multi-descent canyoning week that combines technical instruction and progression across Guara's canyons from a base near Alquezar.
    https://www.expediciones-sc.es/en/
  • Vertientes Aventura
    Canyoning courses, Sierra de Guara
    An Alquezar-based adventure operator offering guided canyoning and multi-day canyoning packages through the Rio Vero, Peonera and other Sierra de Guara canyons with technical abseiling instruction.
    https://www.vertientesaventura.com/?lang=en
  • Guías Boira
    Curso de Barranquismo Sierra de Guara (2-day canyoning course)
    A mountain-guide company with two decades in the Sierra de Guara running a two-day hands-on canyoning course covering rope work, rappelling, handrails, moving-water progression and risk management, with optional rural-hostel accommodation in Alquezar.
    https://www.guiasboira.com/en/barranquismo-guara-pirineo/curso-barranquismo/
