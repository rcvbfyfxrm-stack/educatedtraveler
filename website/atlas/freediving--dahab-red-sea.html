Schools in Dahab (Red Sea)

  • Freedive Dahab
    Beginner to Instructor Package / Freediving Master Program (2-4+ weeks)
    Freedive Dahab is an SSI freediving school founded in 2003 by former world record holder Lotta Ericson, offering a Beginner-to-Instructor pathway and a 2-to-4-week Master Program training at Dahab's Lighthouse and Blue Hole sites.
    https://freedivedahab.com/
  • Dahab Freedivers
    AIDA Instructor Course (requires AIDA 4)
    Dahab Freedivers is an AIDA freediving school in Dahab running courses from AIDA 1 through the AIDA Instructor Course, plus a 2-to-4-week non-certifying Master Program with multiple coaching sessions weekly.
    https://www.dahabfreedivers.com/
  • Touchdown Freediving
    Ultimate Freediver (one-month intensive)
    Touchdown (Touchdown Space) is a Dahab freediving centre founded by Lithuanian record holder Gus Kreivenas, whose one-month Ultimate Freediver intensive combines 10 lectures, 25 dry sessions and 18 water sessions at the Blue Hole.
    https://touchdown-space.com/
