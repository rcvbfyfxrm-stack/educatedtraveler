Schools in London

  • Royal Academy Schools
    Three-year postgraduate programme in contemporary fine art practice
    The RA Schools runs a tuition-free three-year postgraduate fine-art programme in central London with staffed intaglio, relief and screen-printing studios integrated into each student's practice.
    https://www.royalacademy.org.uk/page/the-ra-schools
  • Thames-Side Print Studio
    Printmaking short courses (etching, relief, screenprint, litho)
    Thames-Side Print Studio is an open-access printmaking workshop in Greenwich SE18 offering short courses and keyholder membership across screenprinting, etching, relief and offset lithography.
    https://thamessideprintstudio.wordpress.com/
  • Slade School of Fine Art (UCL)
    MFA Fine Art (Fine Art Media, including printmaking)
    The Slade School of Fine Art at UCL runs an 18-month practice-based MFA whose Fine Art Media facilities include screen printing, etching and stone lithography taught by practising artists.
    https://www.ucl.ac.uk/slade/
  • City & Guilds of London Art School
    BA (Hons) Fine Art with Print specialism
    City & Guilds of London Art School offers BA and MA Fine Art with a print specialist route, taught in a 19th-century Engraving Room focused on traditional intaglio processes including aquatint and colour etching.
    https://www.cityandguildsartschool.ac.uk/
