Schools in Chiang Mai

  • Wat Ram Poeng (Tapotaram) Northern Insight Meditation Centre
    26-day basic Vipassana course (Mahasi method)
    Wat Ram Poeng (Tapotaram), the Northern Insight Meditation Centre in Chiang Mai, runs an ongoing 26-day basic Vipassana course in the Mahasi tradition under teacher guidance, with silent sitting and walking practice from before dawn and follow-on 10-day retreats for graduates.
    https://www.watrampoeng.com/
  • Wat Suan Dok (Monk Chat / meditation retreats)
    Two-day residential meditation retreat for foreigners (MCU Monk Chat)
    Wat Suan Dok in Chiang Mai, through the Mahachulalongkornrajavidyalaya University Monk Chat programme, offers English-language Buddhism conversations and short residential meditation retreats for foreigners, including a weekly two-day course and a longer three-day course at month's end.
    https://www.monkchat.net/
