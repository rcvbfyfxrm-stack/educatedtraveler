Schools in Patagonia / Buenos Aires

  • Francis Mallmann — Wild Cooking in Argentina
    Wild Cooking in Argentina with Francis Mallmann
    A six-night residential cooking experience on Francis Mallmann's private island, La Isla, on Lago La Plata in Patagonia, for a small group of about eight to ten guests.
    https://www.plansouthamerica.com/francis-mallmann-island-argentina/
  • Francis Mallmann (La Isla, Lago La Plata)
    Wild Cooking Experience with Francis Mallmann
    A six-night residential trip to Francis Mallmann's private Patagonian island on Lago La Plata, booked through operator Plan South America, with five hands-on open-fire cooking classes taught by Mallmann.
    https://www.plansouthamerica.com/francis-mallmann-island-argentina/
  • Estancia Nibepo Aike (El Calafate)
    Estancia day visit / lodge stay with traditional Patagonian lamb asado
    A working century-old sheep ranch beside Lago Roca in Los Glaciares National Park near El Calafate, offering day visits and a 10-room lodge stay built around a traditional Patagonian lamb asado.
    https://nibepoaike.com.ar/en/inicio-english/
  • The Asado Experience (Buenos Aires)
    The Asado Experience
    A women-run, three-hour hands-on asado experience held in a century-old private home in the Belgrano R neighborhood of Buenos Aires, where guests take part in the fire and preparation of Argentine beef.
    https://www.theasadoexperience.com/
  • AsadoAdventure (Buenos Aires)
    The Original AsadoAdventure Tour
    A five-hour small-group asado tour in Buenos Aires that includes a Palermo butcher walk and hands-on open-fire grilling and chimichurri-making in a private 1890s home.
    https://www.asadoadventure.com/asadoadventure
