Schools in Ericeira

  • Ericeira Surf Camp
    Surf package combining accommodation, lessons and activities (weekly)
    An accredited surf camp and school in the centre of Ericeira village whose instructors are certified by the Portuguese Surf Federation, offering surf packages that combine accommodation with lessons and options such as yoga or board shaping.
    https://ericeirasurfcamp.com/
  • Lapoint Surf Camp Ericeira
    Weekly surf camp with graded lessons (levels 1-4) and surf guiding
    Set in a converted winery near the beach, this Lapoint camp runs weekly stays with graded lessons across levels 1 to 4 plus surf guiding, alongside a pool, yoga shala and co-working space.
    https://www.lapointcamps.com/surfcamp/portugal/ericeira/
  • Rapture Surfcamp Ericeira
    Weekly beginner-to-progression surf camp
    Housed in a two-storey Portuguese villa overlooking Foz do Lizandro beach, this camp runs daily lessons that progress from complete beginner upward, with added yoga and SUP sessions.
    https://www.rapturecamps.com/surfcamp/portugal/ericeira-lizandro/
