Schools in Arles

  • École Nationale Supérieure de la Photographie (ENSP)
    Master-level three-year photography diploma
    France's only public art school devoted solely to photography, based in Arles since 1982, running a three-year Master-conferring programme combining technical training and theory, plus a continuing-education centre for working photographers.
    https://www.ensp-arles.fr/
  • Rencontres d'Arles workshops
    Photo Workshops (spring and summer, 2-6 days)
    The summer photography festival's year-round workshop programme in Arles, offering around 30 four-to-six-day summer and shorter weekend workshops led by professional photographers, plus Photo Folio portfolio reviews during opening week.
    https://www.rencontres-arles.com/en/photo-workshops-and-photo-folio-review
  • Magnum Photos workshops (Arles)
    Magnum Learn workshops, talks and portfolio reviews
    The Magnum agency's education arm runs photographer-led workshops, talks and free portfolio reviews, including programming staged in Arles around its general meeting and the Rencontres festival; Arles-specific multi-day courses are occasional rather than fixed.
    https://www.magnumphotos.com/event/
