Schools in Chiang Mai

  • Thai Farm Cooking School
    Full-day cooking class at the organic farm
    Operating since 2001 on an organic farm about 17 km outside Chiang Mai, this school runs a full-day class with a local market visit, farm ingredient-picking and making curry paste, curry, soup, stir-fry, a noodle dish and dessert from scratch.
    https://thaifarmcooking.com/
  • Asia Scenic Thai Cooking School
    Full course at the organic farm
    Run by Gayle and family since 2007 with both an Old City garden and a countryside organic farm, Asia Scenic's full farm course teaches six dishes amid 20-plus home-grown vegetables and sends students home with a recipe book.
    https://www.asiascenic.com/
  • Thai Akha Kitchen
    Morning cooking class with market tour (full 11-dish menu)
    Run by members of the Akha hill-tribe in an open-air bamboo kitchen south of Chiang Mai's old city, this school's market-tour class has each student cook an 11-dish Thai-Akha menu at their own wok and mortar station.
    https://www.thaiakhakitchen.com/
