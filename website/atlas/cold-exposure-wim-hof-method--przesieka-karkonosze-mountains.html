Schools in Przesieka, Karkonosze Mountains

  • Wim Hof Method Academy
    WHM Instructor Academy - Module III (in-person intensive)
    The official instructor-certification pathway culminates in a multi-day in-person intensive held at the Academy base in Przesieka, in the Karkonosze mountains where Wim Hof trains.
    https://www.wimhofmethod.com/academy
  • Wim Hof Method Academy (Module III, Przesieka)
    WHM Instructor Academy Module III (Master Module)
    The official Wim Hof Method instructor-certification track culminates in the five-day, in-person Module III Master Module held in Przesieka, Poland, where candidates do repeated ice baths, breath holds, and cold swims and are evaluated teaching practice sessions before being certified.
    https://www.wimhofmethod.com/academy
  • Official Wim Hof Winter Expedition
    Wim Hof Winter Expedition
    The Winter Expedition is a week-long training camp led by Wim Hof in the Karkonosze region around Karpacz, Poland, combining breathing sessions, cold training in natural streams, and a culminating climb of a snowy mountain peak.
    https://www.wimhofmethod.com/wim-hof-expeditions
  • Wim Hof Method Travel
    Wim Hof Method Travel week-long programs
    Wim Hof Method Travel offers official week-long, in-person training programs combining breathing technique, cold exposure, and mindset work at locations such as the WHM Center in the French Alps.
    https://www.wimhofmethod.com/experience-wim-hof
