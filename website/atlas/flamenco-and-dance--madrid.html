Schools in Madrid

  • Amor de Dios
    Intensive flamenco dance courses (cursos intensivos)
    Amor de Dios is a long-running Madrid flamenco and Spanish-dance centre in Lavapiés offering daily classes and intensive courses in dance, cante and guitar, with alumni including Sara Baras and Joaquín Cortés.
    https://flamencomadrid.com/en/schools/amor-de-dios/
  • Real Conservatorio Profesional de Danza Mariemma
    Professional Grade in Spanish Dance (Flamenco specialism)
    The Real Conservatorio Profesional de Danza Mariemma is Madrid's public professional dance conservatory awarding the official Título Profesional, with a six-year Spanish Dance grade covering escuela bolera, folklore, stylized dance and flamenco.
    https://rcpdmariemma.com/
  • Casa Patas (Fundación Conservatorio Flamenco Casa Patas)
    Flamenco dance, cante, guitar and compás classes
    The Fundación Conservatorio Flamenco Casa Patas, a non-profit founded in 2000, continues teaching flamenco dance, cante, guitar, cajón and compás in Madrid in English or Spanish after the associated tablao closed.
    https://casapatas.com/clases-de-flamenco-en-madrid/
