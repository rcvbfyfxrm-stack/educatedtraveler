Schools in Phuket (Chalong)

  • Tiger Muay Thai
    Multi-day Muay Thai, MMA and fitness training packages with on-site accommodation; 150+ classes weekly across all levels
    Tiger Muay Thai is a large training camp opened in 2003 on Soi Ta-iad in Chalong, Phuket, running over 150 Muay Thai, MMA and fitness classes per week for everyone from beginners to professional fighters.
    https://www.tigermuaythai.com/
  • Phuket Top Team
    Multi-disciplinary training camp (Muay Thai, MMA, boxing, wrestling, BJJ) with multi-week packages
    Phuket Top Team is a Chalong training camp on Chaofa East Road offering Muay Thai, boxing, wrestling, BJJ and MMA, with two Muay Thai rings, a full-size MMA cage, ice bath and sauna.
    https://www.phukettopteam.com/
  • Sinbi Muay Thai
    Multi-day Muay Thai training with on-site Sinbi Apartments lodging (group and private sessions, all levels)
    Sinbi Muay Thai is an open-air camp in Rawai near Nai Harn beach in southern Phuket with three full-size rings, offering group and private training plus a 21-room on-site apartment for residential stays.
    https://www.sinbimuaythai.com/
