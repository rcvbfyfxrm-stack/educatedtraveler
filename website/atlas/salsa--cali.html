Where it is taught — hand-verified

Schools in Cali

  • Swing Latino
    Formalized salsa caleña training program (monthly class blocks)
    Cali's well-known competition-level salsa academy founded by Luis Eduardo Hernandez 'El Mulato', running a structured, formalized training program focused on the fast-footwork salsa caleña style.
    https://www.instagram.com/swinglatinoacademy/
  • Son de Luz
    Salsa caleña classes for visitors plus group and private dance programs
    A Cali dance school and artistic foundation directed by Luz Aide Moncayo that teaches salsa caleña and other Latin styles to all ages and nationalities, with classes geared to both residents and visiting dancers.
    https://sondeluz.co/
  • Arrebato Caleño
    Social salsa caleña classes for foreigners with cultural-exchange immersion
    A San Antonio salsa school built around connecting foreign visitors and locals, offering personalized and group social salsa caleña classes plus cultural-exchange activities around the city's dance scene.
    https://arrebatocaleno.com/
  • Academia Latin Fever
    Salsa caleña and social dance classes for all ages and nationalities
    A Cali dance academy founded in 2006 by Gustavo A. Collazos in the San Fernando Viejo neighborhood, teaching salsa caleña, salsa en linea, bachata and other social dances to students of all ages and nationalities.
