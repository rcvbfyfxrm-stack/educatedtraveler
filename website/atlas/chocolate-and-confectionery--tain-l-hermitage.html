Schools in Tain-l'Hermitage

  • L'École Valrhona (via École Chocolat residential program)
    Professional Chocolatier Program in France (Tain-l'Hermitage residency)
    A one-week residency (six nights) built around three full days of hands-on training at L'École Valrhona in Tain-l'Hermitage, Valrhona's home in the Drôme.
    https://www.ecolechocolat.com/en/french-chocolate.html
  • L'École Valrhona
    Professional chocolate training courses (Tain-l'Hermitage)
    Valrhona's professional training school, founded in 1989 at its Drôme headquarters, offering two- and three-day vocational courses in chocolate, pastry, baking, ice cream and plated desserts for working pastry chefs and chocolatiers.
    https://www.valrhona.com/en/l-ecole-valrhona/our-professional-classes/professional-classes
  • Cité du Chocolat Valrhona (workshops)
    Hands-on pastry and chocolate-making workshops
    Valrhona's 2,000 m² interactive chocolate discovery centre on the site of its historic Tain-l'Hermitage factory, offering short 30-minute to 2-hour hands-on pastry and chocolate workshops led by a Valrhona pastry chef.
    https://citeduchocolat.com/en/workshops
