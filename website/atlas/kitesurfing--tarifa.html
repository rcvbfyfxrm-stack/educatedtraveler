Schools in Tarifa

  • Freeride Tarifa
    IKO kitesurf course (max 2-4 students per instructor)
    An IKO-affiliated kite school teaching at the Los Lances and Valdevaqueros beaches with a maximum of two to four students per instructor and radio-headset instruction.
    https://freeridetarifa.com/
  • Matos Tarifa
    IKO kitesurf courses, beginner to advanced
    A Tarifa kite school whose instructors are certified by the IKO and the Andalusian Sailing Federation, teaching levels from beginner discovery to advanced strapless using radio-helmet instruction.
    https://matos-tarifa.com/en/
  • Subcielo Kite School
    IKO-certified group, semi-private and private kitesurf courses
    Operating in Tarifa for over 20 years from the Valdevaqueros beach, this IKO-certified school offers group, semi-private and private courses with an IKO certificate on completion.
    https://www.subcielokiteschooltarifa.com/en/
  • KBC (Kiteboarding Club) Tarifa
    IKO kitesurf courses with radio instruction
    Located at Los Lances Norte in Tarifa, this kite school uses IKO-certified coaches and radio training, with kitesurf and wingfoil courses plus an associated hostel.
    https://www.kiteboarding-club.com/en/tarifa/
