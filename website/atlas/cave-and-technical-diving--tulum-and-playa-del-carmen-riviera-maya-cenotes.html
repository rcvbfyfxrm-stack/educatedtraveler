Schools in Tulum & Playa del Carmen (Riviera Maya cenotes)

  • ProTec Dive Centers
    Full Cave diving and KISS Sidewinder rebreather courses
    ProTec Dive Centers, operating in Playa del Carmen and Tulum since 2001, teaches cave diving and is Mexico's largest hub for factory-approved KISS Sidewinder rebreather training, minutes from the Sac-Actun and Ox-Bel-Ha systems.
    https://protecdivecenters.com/
  • Zero Gravity
    GUE Cave 1 and Cave 2 (instructor-trainer level)
    Zero Gravity is a Puerto Aventuras cave-diving center between Playa del Carmen and Tulum that serves as the regional GUE hub with instructor-trainers, offering GUE cave courses and on-site accommodation steps from numerous cenotes.
    https://zerogravity.com.mx/
  • Under The Jungle
    Cave diving training and annual cave photography workshop
    Under The Jungle is a cave-diving center based at Cenote Tajma Ha in the Riviera Maya offering cave training, cave guiding and an annual multi-day cave photography workshop.
    https://www.underthejungle.com/en/home/
