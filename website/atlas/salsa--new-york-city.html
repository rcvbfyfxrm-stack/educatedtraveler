Schools in New York City

  • Eddie Torres Dance Studio / EddieTorresNY
    Progressive on-clock New York-style mambo/salsa classes with Eddie and Maria Torres
    The Manhattan studio of Eddie 'Mambo King' Torres and Maria Torres, who codified New York-style on-2 salsa, offering leveled mambo and salsa classes several nights a week plus a children's program.
    https://www.eddietorresny.com/
  • Santo Rico Dance
    Leveled in-studio salsa/mambo training (beginner through advanced) under Tomas Guerrero
    A New York salsa and mambo school and performance company led by Tomas Guerrero, offering a structured progression of leveled classes focused on timing, technique and partner connection at its East Harlem studio.
    https://santoricodance.com/
  • Piel Canela Dance
    Unlimited multi-style group classes (salsa, mambo, bachata and more) with a 30-day unlimited-access option for new students
    A nonprofit Latin dance school operating in Midtown Manhattan since 2000, directed by Joe Burgos and Bianca Soto, teaching salsa, mambo, bachata and many other styles across levels with an unlimited-class membership model.
    https://pielcaneladancers.com/
