Schools in Sjoa (Heidal Valley)

  • Mad Goats Norway
    Sjoa Kayak Camp
    A Sjoa-based paddling outfit whose multi-day Sjoa Kayak Camp trains beginner through advanced whitewater kayakers across different river sections from a riverside base with sauna and camping.
    https://madgoats.no/
  • Sjoa Kajakksenter
    River kayak courses (beginner / advanced)
    A Heidal kayak school running since 1997, offering multi-day river kayaking courses for beginners and advanced paddlers with BCU/ACA/Rescue-3-certified instructors in small groups.
    https://www.kajakksenteret.no/sjoa-kajakksenter-kayak-courses_223.html
  • Heidal Rafting
    Whitewater kayaking courses
    A Sjoa Valley adventure centre operating since 1992 that offers whitewater kayaking courses and tours on the Grade 2-3 Sjoa, Laagen and Otta rivers with on-site accommodation and meals.
    https://heidalraftingisjoa.no/?lang=en
