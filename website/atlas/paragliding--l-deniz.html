Schools in Ölüdeniz

  • Sky Sports Paragliding
    SIV course
    Operating at Oludeniz since 1991, Sky Sports flies tandem descents from Babadag and runs an SIV course (open to pilots with at least 100 flights and a declared license) covering theory plus instructor-guided recovery from unexpected in-flight situations.
    https://skysports-turkey.com/
  • Gravity Tandem Paragliding
    Tandem flight from Babadag (1960 m)
    Gravity is one of the largest paragliding operators at Oludeniz, launching from the 1960 m Babadag flight sites for 25-45 minute tandem descents to the beach, but its public offering is tandem flights rather than a residential pilot-certification program.
    https://flygravity.com/
  • Easy Riders Paragliding
    Easy Riders is an Oludeniz multi-activity operator that books tandem paragliding alongside boat trips and other excursions, but no dedicated paragliding-school website or structured immersive pilot course could be verified.
