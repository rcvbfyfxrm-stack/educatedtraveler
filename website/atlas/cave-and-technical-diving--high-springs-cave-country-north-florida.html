Schools in High Springs (Cave Country, North Florida)

  • Global Underwater Explorers (GUE)
    GUE Cave Diver Level 1
    The entry point into GUE's cave progression, run over six days across the North Florida springs (Ginnie Springs, Devil's, Peacock and others around High Springs).
    https://www.gue.com/diver-training/explore-gue-courses/technical/cave-1
  • Cave Country Dive Shop
    Cave diving training (Cavern through Full Cave and Cave CCR via NSS-CDS / NACD / TDI)
    Cave Country Dive Shop is a full-service shop in High Springs, Florida whose cave-instructor team teaches overhead training from cavern up to full cave and cave CCR through the NSS-CDS, NACD and TDI/SDI agencies.
    https://www.cavecountrydiving.com/
  • Wet Rocks Diving
    GUE Cave 1 and Cave 2 training and guided cave diving
    Wet Rocks Diving, led by GUE instructor Meredith Tanguay, provides GUE Cave 1 and Cave 2 training and professional cave guiding across North Florida's High Springs spring-and-cave systems.
    https://www.wetrocksdiving.com/
