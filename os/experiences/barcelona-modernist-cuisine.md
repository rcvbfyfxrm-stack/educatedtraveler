## 5-Day Programme
| Day | Topic | Key Techniques |
|-----|-------|---------------|
| 1 | Sous-Vide & Low Temperature | Vacuum sealing, precision temp, compression marinades, texture transformation |
| 2 | Textures & Hydrocolloids | Agar, gellan, xanthan, methylcellulose, firm/fluid/elastic gels, crispy textures |
| 3 | Spherification & Encapsulation | Basic/reverse/frozen spherification, caviar pearls, encapsulated sauces |
| 4 | Foams, Espumas & Siphon | ISI siphon, hot/cold foams, lecithin airs, CO₂ carbonation |
| 5 | Integration & Menu Development | Design 5-course modernist tasting menu, peer critique, final presentation |
