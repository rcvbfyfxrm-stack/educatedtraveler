## Curriculum

### Level 1 — Basic
- **Hands-on:** Knife handling, sushi rice (shari), fish fillets (saku), seafood prep, nigiri, hosomaki
- **Theory:** Sushi culture & history, key ingredients, knife maintenance
- **Exam:** Complete sushi plate — technique, balance, presentation

### Level 2 — Intermediate
- **Hands-on:** Fish butchery & filleting, futomaki & uramaki, sashimi cutting & plating, counter etiquette
- **Theory:** Fish identification, seasonality, ingredient quality
- **Exam:** Serve custom orders at the counter (simulated sushi bar)

### Level 3 — Superior
- **Hands-on:** Delicate fish (anago, hirame), otsumami, creative nigiri, full omakase course
- **Theory:** Omakase structure, course balance, guest experience, fish market visit
- **Exam:** Present and serve a complete omakase at the counter
