## Partner Vetting Checklist

Before approaching any partner, verify:

1. **Certification Authority** — Recognized by international bodies (PADI, RYA, WSET, Yoga Alliance, etc.)
2. **Track Record** — 5+ years operating, verifiable graduates
3. **Safety Record** — No major incidents, proper insurance
4. **Reputation** — Online reviews, industry recognition, awards
5. **Values Alignment** — Sustainability, authenticity, quality focus
6. **Capacity** — Can handle our cohort sizes (8-12 students)
7. **English Capability** — Or interpreter arrangements
8. **Accommodation Quality** — Meets our standards or we arrange separately

## Approaching Custom Instructors

For "Custom" arrangements:

1. Reach out through professional networks
2. Request references from previous students
3. Arrange video call interview
4. Negotiate exclusive or priority arrangements
5. Create formal partnership agreement
6. Test with pilot cohort before full commitment
