## How to Use This Tracker

1. **Before outreach**: Move partner from ⚪ to 🔴, add First Contact date
2. **After response**: Update status to 🟡, add Last Contact and Notes
3. **Set follow-ups**: Always fill Next Action with specific date/task
4. **Weekly review**: Check all 🔴 partners older than 7 days for follow-up
5. **Update summary**: Keep the count table current
