# Survey Engagement Strategies
## Creating Emotional Connection + Perceived Benefit

**Goal**: Get people to take the 2-minute survey by making them feel something AND see what's in it for them.

---

## The Psychology Framework

### Three Emotional Triggers

1. **Identity Activation** — "This is who I want to become"
2. **FOMO + Exclusivity** — "I want to be part of building this"
3. **Curiosity Gap** — "I need to know what this is about"

### The Benefit Ladder

| Level | Message |
|-------|---------|
| Immediate | "Shape what we build" |
| Personal | "Tell us YOUR dream skill adventure" |
| Social | "Join 500+ people designing their ideal escape" |
| Exclusive | "Early supporters get priority access" |

## TESTING FRAMEWORK

**Week 1-2:** Test 3 variants per platform
**Metrics:** Click-through rate, survey completion rate, time on survey

**Winning Formula:**
```
[Emotional Hook] + [Clear Benefit] + [Low Commitment CTA]
```

Example:
"What skill would change your life?" (Identity)
+ "Your answers shape what we build" (Participation benefit)
+ "2 minutes. Link in bio." (Low friction)
