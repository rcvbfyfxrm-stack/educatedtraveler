# Student Interview Template
## *Vetting Instructors Through Past Students — Finding World-Class Partners*

Use this template to interview past students of instructors you're considering partnering with. The goal: understand if this instructor delivers the kind of transformative, world-class experience that defines EducatedTraveler.

---

## Interview Framework

### Before the Interview

1. **Research the instructor** — Know their background, specialties, and reputation
2. **Find 3-5 past students** — Through social media, reviews, instructor referrals
3. **Prepare experience-specific questions** — Use the category templates below
4. **Record the conversation** — With permission, for detailed notes

---

## Universal Questions (Ask Everyone)

### Opening — The Decision

1. **Why did you choose [Instructor Name] specifically?**
   - *Looking for: Reputation, recommendation source, unique draw*

2. **What other options did you consider before choosing this instructor/program?**
   - *Looking for: Competitive landscape, what made them stand out*

3. **What were your expectations before starting?**
   - *Looking for: Baseline to measure transformation*

---

### The Experience — Day-to-Day

4. **Describe a typical day training with [Instructor].**
   - *Looking for: Structure, intensity, hands-on time, theory balance*

5. **What was the instructor's teaching style like?**
   - *Looking for: Patient/demanding, traditional/innovative, hands-on/lecture*

6. **How much personal attention did you receive?**
   - *Looking for: Group size, 1-on-1 time, feedback quality*

7. **What was the most challenging moment of your training?**
   - *Looking for: How instructor handled struggles, support level*

8. **What surprised you most about the experience?**
   - *Looking for: Unexpected value, hidden gems*

---

### The Instructor — Character & Passion

9. **What makes [Instructor Name] different from other teachers in this field?**
   - *Looking for: Unique methodology, philosophy, expertise*

10. **Can you describe a moment when you saw [Instructor]'s true passion for the craft?**
    - *Looking for: Authenticity, dedication, love for teaching*

11. **How did [Instructor] handle mistakes or failures during training?**
    - *Looking for: Teaching approach, patience, constructive feedback*

12. **Did [Instructor] share personal stories or history with the craft?**
    - *Looking for: Cultural transmission, depth of experience*

---

### The Transformation

13. **How did you change as a result of this experience?**
    - *Looking for: Skills gained, mindset shifts, confidence*

14. **What can you do now that you couldn't do before?**
    - *Looking for: Tangible skill acquisition*

15. **How has this experience affected your life since returning home?**
    - *Looking for: Lasting impact, career changes, continued practice*

16. **Would you say this was the best training experience of its kind worldwide? Why?**
    - *Looking for: Honest benchmark comparison*

---

### Value Assessment

17. **Was the investment worth it? (time, money, effort)**
    - *Looking for: Honest value assessment*

18. **What would you have paid for this experience knowing what you know now?**
    - *Looking for: Perceived value, pricing guidance*

19. **What would have made the experience even better?**
    - *Looking for: Improvement opportunities, gaps we could fill*

---

### Recommendation

20. **Would you recommend [Instructor] to a serious student? Why?**
    - *Looking for: Enthusiastic yes vs qualified yes vs hesitation*

21. **What type of person would thrive with [Instructor]?**
    - *Looking for: Student profile match*

22. **What type of person should NOT train with [Instructor]?**
    - *Looking for: Red flags, mismatches to avoid*

23. **On a scale of 1-10, how likely are you to recommend this experience to a friend?**
    - *Looking for: Net Promoter Score indicator*

## Experience-Specific Question Modules

### CULINARY EXPERIENCES

**Technical Mastery:**
- How much time did you spend on fundamentals vs advanced techniques?
- Did you learn the "why" behind the techniques, not just the "how"?
- Were you cooking independently by the end, or always supervised?

**Cultural Immersion:**
- Did the training include understanding of ingredients at the source (markets, farms)?
- How much cultural context was provided with the cooking?
- Did you eat what you cooked? How was the feedback process?

**Instructor Credibility:**
- What is [Instructor]'s background in professional kitchens?
- Have they trained other students who are now working professionally?
- Are they connected to the local culinary community?

**The Food:**
- Was what you learned at the highest level of the cuisine?
- Would you serve what you learned to discerning guests with confidence?
- Did you learn secret techniques or family recipes not available elsewhere?

---

### SAILING / WATER EXPERIENCES

**Technical Competency:**
- What conditions did you sail/dive in? Range of weather, water conditions?
- Did you feel confident handling the boat/equipment independently by the end?
- How was safety emphasized? Any emergency scenarios practiced?

**Real-World Application:**
- Did training include actual passages/expeditions, not just protected waters?
- How did [Instructor] handle stressful situations at sea?
- Would you crew for [Instructor] on a long passage?

**Instructor Credibility:**
- What is [Instructor]'s sailing/diving history? Notable expeditions?
- Are their certifications recognized internationally (RYA, PADI, AIDA)?
- How do they stay current in their field?

---

### MARTIAL ARTS / PHYSICAL TRAINING

**Training Intensity:**
- How physically demanding was the training? Hours per day?
- Was there periodization — rest days, recovery focus?
- Did you risk injury? How was safety managed?

**Technical Progression:**
- Could you track your improvement over the training period?
- Was the curriculum structured or improvisational?
- Did you spar/compete during training?

**Lineage & Authenticity:**
- Can [Instructor] trace their lineage to founders/masters?
- Is their style recognized by governing bodies?
- Are they actively training or competing themselves?

---

### WELLNESS / YOGA / MEDITATION

**Depth of Practice:**
- Was this a tourist experience or serious practice?
- How much theory/philosophy vs physical practice?
- Did you experience genuine transformation or just relaxation?

**Instructor Authenticity:**
- What is [Instructor]'s personal practice like? How long have they practiced?
- Do they live the philosophy they teach?
- Did you feel they genuinely cared about your development?

**Safe Container:**
- How were emotional/spiritual challenges handled?
- Was the environment supportive and non-judgmental?
- Did you feel safe to be vulnerable?

---

### TRADITIONAL ARTS & CRAFTS

**Technical Mastery:**
- How long has [Instructor] practiced this craft?
- Are they considered a master by their peers/community?
- Did you learn techniques that take years to develop?

**Cultural Transmission:**
- Did you learn the history and meaning behind the craft?
- Is this a living tradition or museum piece?
- Did [Instructor] share the "soul" of the craft, not just technique?

**Tangible Outcome:**
- What did you create? Are you proud of it?
- Could you continue practicing independently after training?
- Did [Instructor] help you develop your own style?

---

### ADVENTURE / OUTDOOR SKILLS

**Risk Management:**
- How was safety handled in high-risk situations?
- What certifications does [Instructor] hold (IFMGA, NZMGA, etc.)?
- Any incidents during training? How were they handled?

**Skill Development:**
- Did you progress to more challenging terrain/conditions?
- Can you now operate independently in these environments?
- Would you trust [Instructor] with your life in extreme conditions?

**Local Knowledge:**
- Does [Instructor] know the area intimately?
- Did they share hidden spots, local secrets?
- How long have they operated in this region?

---

### PHOTOGRAPHY / CREATIVE

**Technical Growth:**
- Did your technical skills measurably improve?
- Was the instruction personalized to your level?
- Did you receive meaningful critique on your work?

**Artistic Development:**
- Did [Instructor] help you find your voice/style?
- Was there focus on seeing, not just camera settings?
- Did you produce portfolio-worthy work during training?

**Industry Credibility:**
- What is [Instructor]'s professional background?
- Are they recognized by peers (publications, awards)?
- Do they have connections that could benefit your career?

---

### CONSERVATION / WILDLIFE

**Scientific Credibility:**
- Is [Instructor] connected to legitimate research organizations?
- What publications or credentials do they have?
- Is the work they do peer-recognized?

**Real Impact:**
- Did your participation contribute to actual conservation?
- Was this genuine work or "voluntourism"?
- Can you see measurable outcomes from the program?

**Field Skills:**
- What practical skills did you learn (tracking, data collection, etc.)?
- Would you be qualified for entry-level conservation work after this?
- Did you receive any certifications (FGASA, etc.)?

## Red Flags to Watch For

| Red Flag | What It Means |
|----------|---------------|
| Vague answers about instructor's background | May lack credibility |
| Hesitation on recommendation | Hidden problems |
| "It was fine" with no enthusiasm | Mediocre experience |
| Emphasis on facilities over instructor | Not instructor-driven |
| Couldn't describe transformation | No real impact |
| Safety concerns mentioned | Risk to our students |
| "I wouldn't do it again" | Obvious rejection |
| Price complaints combined with other issues | Not about value |

---

## Green Flags — Signs of Excellence

| Green Flag | What It Means |
|------------|---------------|
| Unprompted enthusiasm | Genuine love for the experience |
| Specific transformation stories | Real impact occurred |
| "Changed my life" language | Deep transformation |
| Would pay more knowing what they know | High perceived value |
| Want to return or continue training | Loyalty indicator |
| Recommend without hesitation | Strong endorsement |
| Specific praise for instructor's character | Personal connection |
| Still in touch with instructor | Relationship beyond transaction |

## Scoring Guide

After the interview, rate the following (1-5):

| Criterion | Score | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| **Instructor Expertise** | /5 | Technical mastery, credentials |
| **Teaching Ability** | /5 | Communication, patience, structure |
| **Passion & Authenticity** | /5 | Love for craft, genuine care |
| **Student Transformation** | /5 | Measurable change achieved |
| **Uniqueness** | /5 | Not available elsewhere |
| **Value Delivered** | /5 | Worth the investment |
| **Safety & Professionalism** | /5 | Risk management, reliability |
| **Cultural Depth** | /5 | Beyond technique, true immersion |
| **TOTAL** | /40 | |

**Scoring Interpretation:**
- **35-40**: World-class partner — pursue immediately
- **28-34**: Strong potential — worth exploring further
- **20-27**: Average — not EducatedTraveler quality
- **Below 20**: Pass — does not meet standards

---

## Post-Interview Actions

1. **Document everything** — Full notes within 24 hours
2. **Cross-reference** — Compare across multiple students
3. **Verify claims** — Check credentials, certifications, history
4. **Site visit** — If score is 28+, schedule in-person evaluation
5. **Pilot cohort** — Before full partnership, run test group
