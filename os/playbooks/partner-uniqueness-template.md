# Partner Experience Uniqueness Template
## *Research Framework for Personalized Outreach*

Use this framework to research and highlight what makes each partner's offering stand out. Tailor the opening paragraph in outreach emails for personalization and credibility.

**Research Sources:** Partner website, testimonials, certification body listings (RYA, PADI, Yoga Alliance, WSET), social media, reviews.

---

## Step 1: Key Research Areas

### Universal Categories (All Partners)

| Category | Prompts/Questions |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Instructor Expertise** | Lead instructor qualifications? Unique background (competitions, expeditions, celebrity clients)? Years instructing? Notable students? |
| **Program Structure** | Duration options? Max students/cohort? Hands-on vs. theory ratio? Progressive levels? |
| **Location/Conditions** | What makes this location authentic/ideal? Unique geography? Seasonal advantages? |
| **Facilities/Equipment** | Quality of equipment? Modern/traditional? Unique amenities? |
| **Unique Selling Points** | What do they offer that others don't? Signature modules? Industry connections? |
| **Outcomes** | Certification types? Pass rates? Alumni success stories? Career placements? |

## Step 3: Email Opener Formula

**Structure:**
> "I've long admired [Partner's Signature Program]—[Unique Metric/Feature] under [Key Person's Cred], [Specific Edge], yielding [Outcome]. [Highlight 2nd Strength]."

**Components:**
1. **Signature Program** - Their flagship offering
2. **Unique Metric** - Specific number (72 sea days, 8,000 dives, 30 years)
3. **Key Person's Cred** - Lead instructor's notable qualification
4. **Specific Edge** - What sets them apart
5. **Outcome** - Results they deliver
6. **2nd Strength** - Reinforcing detail

---

## Step 4: Validation Checklist

Before sending outreach:

- [ ] 2-3 verifiable specifics (cite web page/source)
- [ ] Ties to certification body metrics (pass rates, logged hours/miles)
- [ ] Avoid generics ("best school," "amazing experience")—use "72 days," "8-student max," "America's Cup"
- [ ] Length: 1-2 sentences, scannable
- [ ] Fact-checked against their website
- [ ] Personalized to specific instructor if possible
