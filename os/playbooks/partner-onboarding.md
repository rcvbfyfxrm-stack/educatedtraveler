# Partner Onboarding Playbook

Templates and frameworks for bringing partners into the EducatedTraveler ecosystem.

---

## Partner Types

| Type | What They Provide | What They Receive |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Instructors** | Expertise, teaching | Fee + bonuses + visibility |
| **Venues** | Accommodation, space | Guaranteed bookings |
| **Certification Bodies** | Official credentials | High-intent learners |
| **Destinations** | Local connections | Premium positioning |

## Instructor Partnership

### Requirements
- [ ] 5+ years professional experience
- [ ] Active practitioner (still doing the work)
- [ ] Certified by recognized body
- [ ] Strong English communication
- [ ] Willingness to be filmed for marketing
- [ ] CPR/First Aid certified

### Compensation Package

**Base Fees**:
| Duration | Fee Range |
|----------|-----------|
| 7-14 days | $1,500-$2,500 |
| 21-30 days | $3,000-$4,500 |
| 60 days | $6,000-$8,000 |
| 90+ days | $10,000-$15,000 |

**Per-Student Bonus**: $150-$500 depending on duration
**Repeat Bonus**: +15% for returning instructors
**NPS Bonus**: +$500 if cohort NPS > 80

**We Cover**: Flights, accommodation, meals, insurance

## Venue Partnership

### Requirements
- [ ] Premium quality (4-star equivalent minimum)
- [ ] Capacity for 8-12 guests
- [ ] Private or semi-private accommodations
- [ ] Communal spaces for group work
- [ ] Outdoor areas for movement
- [ ] Stable WiFi
- [ ] Flexible cancellation terms
- [ ] Authentic location (source matters)

### Partnership Models

**Model A: Revenue Share**
- 10-15% of accommodation value
- Best for: Premium properties wanting exposure

**Model B: Fixed Rate**
- 20% below rack rate
- Guaranteed occupancy (low season especially)
- Best for: Properties seeking predictable revenue

**Model C: Equity Partnership**
- Exclusive access in exchange for equity stake
- Best for: Unique properties, long-term alignment

## Red Flags

Do not proceed if:
- Requests upfront payment from us
- Unwilling to be filmed/photographed
- No verifiable credentials
- Poor communication response times
- Negative references
- Prioritizes volume over quality
- Inflexible on dates/terms
