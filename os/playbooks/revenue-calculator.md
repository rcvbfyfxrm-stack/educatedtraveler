Templates for modeling revenue from new partners, products, and cohorts.

## Quick Cohort Calculator

### Inputs
```
Program Name:     _______________
Duration:         _____ days
Location:         _______________
Price per person: $_______________
Cohort size:      _____ (min 8, max 12)
Cohorts per year: _____
```

### Cost Estimation

| Cost Item | Calculation | Amount |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Instructor Fee | Base + (participants × bonus) | $_____ |
| Accommodation | $40-80/night × nights × participants | $_____ |
| Meals | $20/day × days × participants | $_____ |
| Materials & Cert | $25-50/participant | $_____ |
| Transport/Activities | $200-500/participant | $_____ |
| Marketing (5%) | 5% of revenue | $_____ |
| Contingency (5%) | 5% of costs | $_____ |
| **Total Cost** | | **$_____** |

### Revenue Calculation

```
REVENUE
Price × Participants = $_____

COSTS
(from above) = $_____

GROSS PROFIT
Revenue - Costs = $_____

MARGIN
(Gross Profit ÷ Revenue) × 100 = ____%

ANNUAL PROJECTION
Gross Profit × Cohorts per year = $_____
```

## Worked Example: New Freediving Program

### Inputs
```
Program Name:     Freediving Dahab
Duration:         14 days
Location:         Dahab, Egypt
Price per person: $3,800
Cohort size:      10 (target)
Cohorts per year: 6
```

### Cost Breakdown

| Cost Item | Calculation | Amount |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Instructor Fee | $2,000 + (10 × $150) | $3,500 |
| Accommodation | $35 × 14 × 10 | $4,900 |
| Meals | $15 × 14 × 10 | $2,100 |
| Materials & Cert | $40 × 10 | $400 |
| Transport/Activities | $300 × 10 | $3,000 |
| Marketing (5%) | 5% × $38,000 | $1,900 |
| Contingency (5%) | 5% × $13,900 | $695 |
| **Total Cost** | | **$16,495** |

### Revenue Calculation

```
REVENUE
$3,800 × 10 = $38,000

COSTS
$16,495

GROSS PROFIT
$38,000 - $16,495 = $21,505

MARGIN
($21,505 ÷ $38,000) × 100 = 56.6%

ANNUAL PROJECTION
$21,505 × 6 cohorts = $129,030
```

## Decision Rules

**Launch if**:
- Margin > 30%
- Breakeven < 6 participants
- Waitlist interest > 20 people
- Instructor secured
- Venue confirmed

**Don't launch if**:
- Margin < 25%
- Breakeven > 7 participants
- No clear certification outcome
- Instructor unvetted
- Location inauthentic
