## The Full Story (5-10 Minutes)

### Opening: The Problem

```
"Let me tell you about two types of regret.

The first is when you're 25, stuck in a job you don't love,
scrolling Instagram, watching someone else sail across an
ocean or make perfect sushi, thinking: 'I wish I could do that.'

The second is when you're 55, with money and time, realizing
you spent 30 years saying 'someday' and someday never came.

Between those two points, most people take the same vacation
over and over. Beach. Pool. Buffet. Photos. Repeat. They
come home with a tan that fades in two weeks and nothing else.

Meanwhile, the skills they dreamed of learning? Still dreams.

Here's why: Learning something real is hard to figure out.

Want to become a certified diver? There's PADI, SSI, NAUI—
which one? Where? How long? Is that instructor any good?

Want to learn to sail? You'd need to research RYA certifications,
find a boat, figure out where to go, hope the captain actually
teaches instead of just using you as crew.

Want to become a yoga teacher? There are 10,000 RYT-200 programs.
Which one is legit? Which one will actually transform you?

Most people give up before they start. The friction is too high.
The logistics are too complex. The risk of wasting time and
money is too real.

So they book another beach resort instead."
```

We design, organize, and run certified skill adventures at

Here's what that looks like:

SUSHI MASTERY — TOKYO — 30 DAYS — $12,000

OCEAN SAILING ODYSSEY — GIBRALTAR TO CANARIES — 21-90 DAYS
You cross the Atlantic, learning celestial navigation,

```
"Compare it to the alternative. A traditional culinary
program costs $50,000+ over 2 years. Self-organizing the
equivalent trip—flights, accommodation, finding an instructor,
hoping it's good—would cost nearly as much and might not work.

Our price includes everything: world-class instruction,
accommodation, meals, certification, cultural experiences.

And you leave with credentials that open doors for life."
```

### "I don't have that much time."
```
"We have options from 7 days to 6 months. Our shortest
certifications—like RYA sailing foundations or WSET wine—
can happen in a week or two.

The question is: do you have time for another vacation
that doesn't change anything?"

### "What if I'm a beginner?"
```
"Most of our participants start as beginners. That's the
point. We don't require experience—we create it.

Our instructors have taught hundreds of people who started
exactly where you are. Beginners welcome. Mastery expected."

### "How is this different from a cooking class abroad?"

We're 30 days of daily training with a master. You learn
the fundamentals, the philosophy, the culture. You leave
with professional certification and skills for life.

It's the difference between trying something and becoming
something."
