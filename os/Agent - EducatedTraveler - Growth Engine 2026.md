## 7. Instagram 2026 — What Actually Moves The Needle

**Algorithm reality (Mosseri + Buffer/Later/Sprout data, May 2026):**

- **Reels reach non-followers 5–10x more than carousels.** Carousels deepen the people already in.
- **Top three ranking signals:** watch-time, likes-per-reach, sends-per-reach. **Shares** is the king signal for new-audience expansion.
- **Saves > likes** for premium offers. High saves = "reference content" = compounding distribution.
- **First 3 seconds = the only thing that matters for Reels reach.**
- **Faceless luxury content is dead.** Founder-on-camera = 30% higher comment rates.
- **Under 50K followers = sweet spot for organic growth.** Stop modeling accounts 100x your size.

### Weekly Pillar Mix (5–6 posts/week)

| Pillar | Cadence | Content | Hook Template |
|--------|---------|---------|---------------|
| **Galley to Cohort** | 3 Reels/wk | 15–30 sec hands-only yacht-chef footage. Fish breakdown, knife work, plate. | Visual cut, no text in s1. |
| **The Tokyo Curriculum** | 2 carousels/wk | 8-slide deep dives — what attendees will master, day by day. | "Day 4: 5 AM at Toyosu. Here's what you actually do." |
| **The Founder Face** | 1 static/wk | Founder portrait + manifesto sentence. The face of the brand. | "I'm a yacht chef. Here's why I'm building this." |

### One DM Trigger, One Flow

Single CTA across all content: **"Reply SUSHI for the September cohort dossier."**

ManyChat → Tally form (email + name + which depth) → Stripe deposit link → Cal.com booking → Notion CRM row → Loom welcome video. Build this once. ~$50/mo total.

### Banned IG Aesthetics For 2026

- Drone-only landscape b-roll (every wellness retreat has it)
- Polished AI-generated thumbnails
- Quote graphics on stock images
- Color-graded sunsets without faces
- "Sign up for my masterclass" sales overlays

**Authentic > polished. iPhone > production. Knife scars > stock images.**

## 15. Crisis Protocol — "It's June 25 and I've sold 3 seats"

This will happen. Plan now.

```
1. STOP — do not panic-discount. Discounting cohort #1 destroys 2027 pricing power.
2. EXTEND THE WINDOW — 7 days only, named publicly: "Two additional priority seats."
3. ACTIVATE LATENT WARMTH — every confirmed student introduces 3 friends. Personal asks.
4. PRIVATE BETA TWO — offer 2 free seats in exchange for testimonial rights (MEA flywheel).
5. SHRINK THE COHORT — 12 → 8 is fine. 8 → 6 is still profitable. <6, postpone gracefully.
6. POST-MORTEM IMMEDIATELY — what assumption broke? Capture in `logs/post-mortems/`.
```

The Daemon law: *truth travels fast.* Better to email the 3 confirmed students that you're tightening to 8 than to ghost the cohort.

## 16. The Decision Filter (When In Doubt)

Every new opportunity goes through five questions, in order. First "no" kills it.

```
1. Does this directly help Sushi Mastery ship full and proud on Sep 21, 2026?
2. Can a 70-hr-yacht-week founder execute this in <2 hrs/week?
3. Does it pass `os/Daemon - EducatedTraveler.md` non-negotiables + the partnership filter?
4. Does it produce a compounding asset (sales page, testimonial reel, evergreen post)?
5. Will I be proud of this when alum #1 references it in their wedding speech?
```

If any answer is no — defer until 2027. The "no" is the moat.
