## Quick Reference

### The Essence
**One week to get certified. 6 months to change your life.**

### The Spectrum
| Duration | Price | Positioning |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| 7-21 days | $3,500-$6,500 | Get certified |
| 30-60 days | $9,500-$12,000 | Go deeper |
| 90-180 days | $25,000-$40,000 | Change your life |

### The Non-Negotiables
- Cohorts: 8-12 max
- Instructors: 5+ years, certified
- Source: Learn where the skill originated
- No sitting blocks > 90 minutes
- Rest days: 1/week minimum

### The Metrics
- Pass rate: 95%+
- NPS: 70+
- Completion: 98%+
- Margin: 30%+

## How to Use

1. **Starting a new initiative?** Check `daemon.md` for mission alignment
2. **Pricing a new program?** Use `economics.md` and `playbooks/revenue-calculator.md`
3. **Recruiting a partner?** Follow `playbooks/partner-onboarding.md`
4. **Need product details?** Reference `products.md`
5. **Building a page?** Use `brand/design-system.md` + `brand/landing-pages.md`
