## Flagship Adventures

### 1. Sushi Mastery — Tokyo
**Duration**: 30 days | **Price**: $12,000 | **XP**: 450

Learn from a master itamae with 30+ years at a Michelin-recognized sushiya. From 5 AM fish market visits to omakase service.

**Curriculum**:
- Week 1: Rice (shari) mastery
- Week 2: Knife work and fish preparation
- Week 3: Nigiri technique
- Week 4: Full omakase service (8-12 courses)

**Certification**: Professional Sushi Chef
**Cohort Size**: 8-12
**Included**: Instruction, knife set (yours to keep), accommodation, breakfast, market visits, cultural excursions

### 2. Sacred Yoga Pilgrimage — Delhi → Rishikesh → Dharamsala
**Duration**: 25-28 days | **Price**: $5,500 | **XP**: 200

The authentic path to RYT-200. Train at the Ganges with E-RYT 500 masters, conclude with Tibetan teachings and potential Dalai Lama blessing.

**Route**:
- Delhi (3 days): Arrival, acclimatization
- Rishikesh (14 days): Core 200-hour training
- Dharamsala (7 days): Tibetan teachings, integration
- Delhi (3 days): Closing ceremony

**Certification**: Yoga Alliance RYT-200 (200+ contact hours)
**Cohort Size**: 10-12
**Included**: All instruction, accommodation (ashram + boutique hotels), vegetarian meals, transport, Dalai Lama teaching access

## Cohort Structure

**Size**: 8-12 participants (6-8 for sailing)

**Timeline**:
- T-90 days: Cohort formation, Slack channel
- T-60 days: Video call with instructor
- T-30 days: Weekly group calls
- T-7 days: WhatsApp group, final prep

**During**:
- Week 1: Foundation + bonding
- Week 2: Depth + challenge
- Week 3+: Mastery + family formation

**After**:
- Cohort channel remains active
- Quarterly regional meetups
- Annual festival (rotating destination)
- Alumni job board
- Mentorship matching
