## Conversion Principles

1. **Lead with transformation, not features**
2. **Use specific examples** (Tokyo, Rishikesh, Gibraltar)
3. **Include credentials** (PADI, RYA, Yoga Alliance)
4. **Show the spectrum** (7 days to 6 months)
5. **Create urgency through exclusivity** (8-12 per cohort)
6. **Always have a clear CTA**
