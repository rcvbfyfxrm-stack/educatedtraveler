## Rules
- Only schools already on the Atlas (hand-verified ones with `confidence: high` first).
- Always credit + tag the school in the post; link their site from the atlas page (already done).
- Written permission (email reply) is enough; keep the thread.
- Never promise placement, ranking, or traffic. The Atlas ranks by community, not by who pays — say so if asked.
