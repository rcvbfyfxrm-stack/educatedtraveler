## Tiered Accommodation

**Problem:** Price sensitivity — full package pricing ($9,500+) is a barrier for some customers.

**Idea:** Offer accommodation tiers instead of removing it entirely.

| Tier | Description | Price Impact |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Shared** | 2-4 person room, communal bathrooms | Base price |
| **Private** | Own room, shared common areas | +$1,500-2,500 |
| **Premium** | Private suite, en-suite bathroom | +$3,000-5,000 |

**Why it works:**
- Preserves cohort living (the moat)
- Lower entry price without becoming "just a course"
- Upsell opportunity for those who want comfort

**To validate:**
- [ ] Survey: "Would you prefer shared or private accommodation?"
- [ ] Research villa/hostel pricing in Bali for different room types
- [ ] Model unit economics at each tier
